# Thamizharivu

**Thamizharivu** is an open initiative to build digital infrastructure for the Tamil language in the age of Artificial Intelligence.

Tamil is one of the world's oldest and richest languages with over 2000 years of literature, philosophy, and knowledge. However, Tamil is underrepresented in modern AI systems and large language models.

This project aims to bridge that gap.

## Mission

Our mission is to create open resources that help researchers, developers, and communities build better tools for Tamil.

We focus on:

- Building open Tamil datasets for AI
- Digitizing Tamil literature and knowledge
- Creating tools for Tamil language processing
- Supporting Tamil language LLM research
- Building a collaborative global Tamil knowledge community

## Why This Project Matters

Most modern AI models are trained heavily on English data. As a result:

- Tamil language tools remain limited
- Tamil knowledge is underrepresented online
- AI systems struggle with Tamil understanding

By building open datasets and tools, we help ensure that Tamil has a strong presence in the AI era.

## What We Are Building

### Tamil Datasets
Curated datasets for training language models.

Examples:
- Tamil literature corpus
- Tamil news datasets
- Tamil conversations
- Parallel translation datasets
- Tamil web text

### Tamil AI Tools

Open-source tools such as:

- Tokenizers
- Transliteration tools
- Tamil search tools
- NLP preprocessing tools

### Tamil Knowledge Archive

Digitizing and preserving:

- Classical Tamil literature
- Modern Tamil writings
- Public domain works

### Tamil LLM Research

Supporting the development of:

- Tamil language models
- Tamil embeddings
- Tamil NLP pipelines
