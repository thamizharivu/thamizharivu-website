# Project Overview

Thamizharivu is an open initiative focused on creating digital infrastructure for the Tamil language.

The goal is to ensure that Tamil language resources are available for the next generation of artificial intelligence technologies.

## Focus Areas

1. Open Tamil datasets
2. Tamil language AI tools
3. Tamil literature digitization
4. Tamil LLM research

## Dataset Goals

We aim to build high-quality datasets such as:

- literature corpora
- conversational datasets
- translation datasets
- knowledge corpora

These datasets will support research in:

- natural language processing
- speech systems
- language models
- translation systems

## Community Collaboration

Thamizharivu is designed as a community-driven project.

Anyone passionate about Tamil language, technology, or research can contribute.

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal is to build a strong digital ecosystem where Tamil can fully participate in the future of artificial intelligence.
