Thamizharivu

Building open digital infrastructure for the Tamil language in the age of Artificial Intelligence.

Our Mission

  • Create open Tamil datasets
  • Digitize Tamil literature
  • Develop Tamil AI tools
  • Support Tamil language model research

Why This Matters

Tamil is one of the oldest languages in the world with a vast literary tradition. However, it is underrepresented in modern AI systems.

Thamizharivu aims to change that by building open resources that help developers, researchers and communities create better Tamil AI tools.

Open Source Project

All work is open and collaborative.
